# Project Description

## Theme
Surviving College Midterms

## Planned Classes
- Player
- Game
- Map
- Student
- Professor

## Limited Resource
- Time
- Sleep

## Time Management
Study period lasts 7 days

## Extra Credit
Character Schedules? TBD

### Planned Implementation
- Given initial time and sleep 
- Gain/lose time and sleep points depending on activity of player
- Track study time
- Need a certian amount of study time to pass classes
- Must meet threshold of sleep and time points in order to survive burnout and cramming 

## Tradeoff System
Losing sleep points for an extension, gives more time 
Losing sleep points for an energy drink, gives more time 

## Mapping Style
Connected map, different college campus locations (library, dining hall, vending machine, dorm, gym)

## Win Condition
Passed classes, didn't end with negative sleep or time

## Lose Condition
Failed a class, burnout (negative sleep), cramming (negative time)
